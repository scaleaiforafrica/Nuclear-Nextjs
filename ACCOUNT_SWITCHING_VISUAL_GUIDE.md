## Summary

The Account Switching feature provides:

1. **Visual Hierarchy**
   - Green for accounts (who you are)
   - Purple for profiles (what role)

2. **Easy Access**
   - Always visible in sidebar
   - One click to switch
   - No logout required

3. **Persistent Sessions**
   - Stored in localStorage
   - Automatic validation
   - Seamless experience

4. **User-Friendly**
   - Add multiple accounts
   - Switch instantly
   - Combine with profile switching
